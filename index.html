What does your web presence look like?

According to research, a business’s online presence can have a massive impact on its success, since most customers will visit a website before deciding to use a service or product. Besides increasing the business credibility, marketing and customer service, your website increases sales and saves you time.

Looking around, I experienced that several of my favourite places do not have a website or have a webpage that underrepresents the quality of their service and product. That is why I decided to help.

How it works?

To deliver my clients' projects with efficiency and peace of mind, a clear process and great communication are key. Hence, I follow the five steps process below, which has been proven to be successful:

  1. Consultation: It all begins with a conversation, where I work with the customer to discover what your needs, wants, and true objectives are. This allows me to plan up front, avoiding costly delays and unforeseen challenges later on
  2. Proposal: Based on our discussion, a detailed project proposal including project timeline and costs will be presented.
  3. Build: After our agreement, I start to build your website. Clients receive regular updates regarding their project status while I create and test it.
  4. Launch: The website is delivered, and I stand ready to make sure that your launch is successful.
  5. Future Support: Once your site launches successfully, I will be available If any future support is needed.
